# Business Overview - GD_python System

## Business Objective

The GD_python system is an advanced platform for automatic diagram generation and document analysis, designed to radically increase the efficiency of business analysts, system architects, and project teams.

## Business Problems

### Current Organizational Challenges

1. **Documentation Creation Time**
   - Manual diagram drawing takes 4-8 hours for medium complexity processes
   - Documentation updates require rework when changes occur
   - Lack of standardization leads to different documentation styles

2. **Quality and Consistency**
   - Different people create documentation in different standards
   - Errors in business process modeling
   - Difficulty maintaining consistency between diagrams

3. **Costs and Resources**
   - High costs of hiring modeling experts
   - Long implementation time for new processes
   - Need for training on modeling tools

4. **Document Analysis**
   - Manual extraction of information from PDF documents
   - Lack of automation in requirements processing
   - Difficulty maintaining context between documents
